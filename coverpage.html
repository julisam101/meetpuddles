Our Story 💭

Built from lived experience, for people who understand

Growing Up With Comfort

Juan and Jorge grew up as first-generation kids in a single-parent household, living paycheck to paycheck. What they had wasn't money—it was family and a few stuffed animals that meant everything.

Those plushies weren't just toys. They were presence. They were something to hold when the night felt long and lonely.

Seeing the Need

Today, loneliness affects everyone. It doesn't matter your age, background, or circumstances. At some point, everyone feels unseen and unsupported.

That's why we created Cuddle Cart. To be that comfort for others. To be the friend someone needs.

Our Mission

"We're not doing this just for ourselves. We're doing this for our families, for people who grew up like us, and for anyone who has ever felt alone."

— Juan & Jorge Lopez, Cuddle Cart 🧸
